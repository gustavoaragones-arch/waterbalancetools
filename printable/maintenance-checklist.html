Pool & Hot Tub Maintenance Checklist

Use this checklist for routine maintenance. For dosing, use WaterBalanceTools.com/calculators/chemical-calculator.html

Daily

  • □ Check chlorine level (pool: 1–3 ppm; hot tub: 3–5 ppm)
  • □ Check pH (7.2–7.6 pool; 7.2–7.8 hot tub)
  • □ Add chlorine or pH adjuster if needed
  • □ Run pump/jets 15–20 min after adding chemicals

Weekly

  • □ Clean skimmer basket
  • □ Test alkalinity
  • □ Adjust chemicals as needed
  • □ Wipe waterline; remove debris

Monthly

  • □ Clean or backwash filter
  • □ Full water test (chlorine, pH, alkalinity)
  • □ Balance water if needed
