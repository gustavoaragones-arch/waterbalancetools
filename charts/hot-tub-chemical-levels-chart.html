Hot Tub Chemical Levels Chart

Hot tubs need slightly different ranges than pools. Use our Hot Tub Chlorine Calculator and pH Calculator for dosing.

Ideal levels for hot tubs

ChemicalIdeal rangeStatus
Free Chlorine3–5 ppmGood
pH7.2–7.8Good
Total Alkalinity80–120 ppmGood

Too high

High chlorine or pH can irritate skin and reduce sanitizer effectiveness. High alkalinity makes pH hard to adjust.

Too low

Low chlorine allows bacteria; low pH can damage equipment and irritate skin. Add chemicals per our hot tub calculators.
