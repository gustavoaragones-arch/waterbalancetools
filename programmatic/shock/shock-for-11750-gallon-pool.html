FAQ

How much shock do I need for my pool?

Use the table above for your pool size. Standard shock raises chlorine by about 10 ppm.

How often should I shock my pool?

Shock weekly or after heavy use, rain, or when chlorine is low. Run the pump 4–6 hours after adding shock.

When can I swim after shocking?

Wait until chlorine returns to 1–3 ppm, usually 24 hours. Test before swimming.
