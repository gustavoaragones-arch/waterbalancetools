How Much Chlorine for a 15,000 Gallon Pool?

Approximate chlorine levelss for a 15,000 gallon pool (from 0 ppm).

Chlorine dosage (to reach target from 0 ppm)

  • To 2 ppm: 0.23 oz liquid chlorine (10%) or 3.0 oz granular shock
  • To 3 ppm: 0.35 oz liquid chlorine (10%) or 4.5 oz granular shock
