FAQ

How much chlorine should a hot tub have?

Keep 3–5 ppm for hot tubs. Test daily and add chlorine as needed.

How often do I add chlorine to a hot tub?

Add chlorine after each use and at least every few days. Run jets 15–20 minutes after adding.
