FAQ

Why would I need to lower pool pH?

If pH is above 7.6, chlorine is less effective and scaling can occur. Use pH reducer (dry acid).

How do I lower pH in my pool?

Use pH reducer according to the table above. Add slowly and re-test after 30 minutes.
