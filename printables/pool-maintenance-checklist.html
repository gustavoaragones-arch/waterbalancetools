Pool Maintenance Checklist

Use our Pool Chlorine Calculator and pH Calculator when adjusting chemicals.

Daily

  • □ Check chlorine (target 1–3 ppm)
  • □ Check pH (target 7.2–7.6)
  • □ Skim debris if needed

Weekly

  • □ Clean skimmer basket
  • □ Test alkalinity
  • □ Adjust chemicals as needed
  • □ Brush walls if needed

Monthly

  • □ Clean or backwash filter
  • □ Full water test (chlorine, pH, alkalinity, CYA)
  • □ Balance water; shock if needed
