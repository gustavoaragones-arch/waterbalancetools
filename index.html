Frequently Asked Questions

What chlorine level should my pool be?

For swimming pools, aim for 1–3 ppm chlorine. For hot tubs and spas, 3–5 ppm is recommended.

What is the ideal pH for pool water?

Keep pH between 7.2 and 7.6 for pools, and 7.2–7.8 for hot tubs. This range is safe for skin and equipment.

How often should I test my pool water?

Test chlorine and pH at least 2–3 times per week. Test alkalinity and other parameters weekly.

Can I use the same chemicals for pools and hot tubs?

Many chemicals are the same, but hot tubs need higher chlorine (3–5 ppm) and you should use spa-specific products when recommended.
